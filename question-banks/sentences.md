# Sentence Bank

## Format
Each line: `English sentence | Chinese sentence`
The app will automatically split and scramble the Chinese characters.

---

I love you | 我爱你
Hello, how are you? | 你好吗怎么你
Thank you very much | 非常谢谢你
I am learning Chinese | 我在学中文
What is your name? | 你叫什么名字
I am hungry | 我饿了
This is very good | 这个很好
I want to eat | 我想吃饭
Good morning | 早上好
I don't understand | 我不懂
Where is the bathroom? | 洗手间在哪里
I am tired | 我累了
Nice to meet you | 很高兴认识你
How old are you? | 你多大
I like this | 我喜欢这个
