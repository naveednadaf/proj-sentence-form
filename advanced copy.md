# Advanced Sentences

I have been studying Chinese for two years | 我学中文两年了
The weather is very nice today | 今天天气很好
I would like to order some food | 我想点一些食物
This restaurant is very famous | 这家餐厅很有名
I need to go to the supermarket | 我需要去超市
She is my best friend | 她是我最好的朋友
We are going to travel next month | 我们下个月要去旅行
He works at a technology company | 他在一家科技公司工作
I enjoy reading Chinese books | 我喜欢读中文书
They are watching a movie now | 他们现在在看电影
